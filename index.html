07

Mistake Log Protocol

1
Mark — During practice, put a ✗ on wrong answers and ? on uncertain ones. Do not re-read yet.
2
Understand — After the session ends, re-read the question. Identify why you got it wrong: concept gap, careless, or unfamiliar format.
3
Log — Open the Mistake Database. Enter subject, mistake description, and the fix/concept to remember.
4
Reinforce — During evening sessions, revisit all logged mistakes. Redo the question without looking at your notes first.
5
Eliminate — Once you can consistently answer a mistake type correctly, mark it as resolved. Target: zero recurring mistakes by exam week.
08

Brutal Rules

🚫
No phone during study sessions. Airplane mode on. No exceptions, no negotiations.
⏰
Morning sessions start at 4:30 AM sharp. Not 4:35. Not after "just one more minute". 4:30.
📝
Every wrong answer gets logged. No skipping. No "I'll remember it". Log it or it never improves.
🛌
In bed by 10 PM. Sleep deprivation destroys memory consolidation. No late nights.
💀
Missing a session activates the Punishment System. No exceptions for "I was tired" or "I forgot".
🔁
Weak topics get double sessions. If you score below 70% on a topic, it goes back to daily rotation.
