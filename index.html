08

Brutal Rules

  • 01Weekdays = full core subject focus. Zero distractions, zero compromises.
  • 02Weekends = core priority + weak/other subjects. Core subjects cannot be skipped under any circumstance.
  • 03Stick strictly to time allocations. Every deviation is marks lost in the exam hall.
  • 04MCQs split into daily chunks. Structured questions rotate chapters and topics every session.
  • 05Sleep, exercise, and nutrition are non-negotiable. They directly determine focus and memory consolidation.

💀 Brutal Truth

  • Weekdays = peak brain time. Bio and Add Maths take the top slot because they demand the most cognitive load. Do not flip this order.
  • Weekends = reinforce core subjects first, then use remaining windows to close the gap on English, BM, and Sejarah.
  • Follow this plan precisely and you will cover every SPM subject efficiently while maintaining maximum marks potential in your priority subjects.
  • Skipping one morning session doesn't just cost that day — it compounds backwards through your mistake log and forward into exam performance.

Punishment System

⚖️ Consequences of Missing Sessions

  • Miss 1 morning session → Add 25 extra MCQs to next day's session. No exceptions.
  • Miss 2 consecutive days → Full chapter re-read of the missed subject + 50 bonus MCQs.
  • Miss 3 days in a week → Weekend has no rest period. Full catch-up sessions Saturday + Sunday morning.
  • Streak breaks → Streak resets to 0. You must earn it back from scratch.
  • Miss evening review → Review that subject twice the next evening (1 hr instead of 30 min).
  • Miss mistake log update → All uncorrected mistakes count double in your database that week.
